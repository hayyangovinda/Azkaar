Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Azkaar. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Islamic remembrance (dhikr) application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Azkaar is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Local Data Storage

Any preferences or progress you make within the app (such as dhikr counts or completed repetitions) are stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Remains on your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Permissions

Azkaar does not request any special permissions from your device. The app operates entirely offline and does not require internet connectivity.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The app functions completely independently without external dependencies.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else. The app is suitable for users of all ages and contains authentic Islamic content.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our app. All app functionality occurs locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and the "Last Updated" date will be revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information for you to access, modify, or delete from our servers. You maintain complete control over any local data by managing your device's app data or uninstalling the application.

Compliance

This app complies with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR and Google Play Store requirements. Our privacy-first approach ensures that your usage of Azkaar remains completely private.
